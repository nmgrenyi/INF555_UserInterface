History

"We will continue to make things the way the grandfathers of our grandfathers did."

Hermes was established by Thierry Hermes in 1837. Originally it was known for the production of harnesses and saddles. The workshop also created wrought iron harnesses and bridles for carriage trade. When it comes to handbags, scarves and fashion, it was only in 1922 when the company introduced its first handbags.

1929 saw the release of the first women’s fashion apparel collection in Paris. It was also in the 1930’s when Hermes produced their famous scarves, and they also opened a scarf factory in 1937. The best of their years include 1946 when they started creating silk ties.

Marketing

"Time is our greatest weapon."

When Hermes decided to keep their classic fonts, their logo, their packaging—they have immortalized the brand, and will remain an inspirational icon in the fashion world. Hermes’ logo will always remind the people that Hermes was once a saddle store and is now the world’s most aspired luxury brand. One thing that sets Hermes apart from their competition is that the brand has preserved the tradition of doing things the way that Thierry Hermes and their grandfathers did back when Hermes was just catering to a limited market.

Material

Hermes designs their bags with only the finest leather materials.

Hermes is known for its handmade luggage and handbags. One bag might require 18 to 24 hours to produce. The construction of each Kelly bag, for example, requires 18 hours to fully realize. Hermes's leathers come from all over the world. Customers may currently wait from six months to one year for delivery of one of the house signature bags.
